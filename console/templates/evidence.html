What this evidence does not establish

Every figure above is a comparison of dated public records: SEC XBRL company facts for the liquidity and cash-flow figures, and ClinicalTrials.gov version history for the registered dates. That comparison is the whole of what is shown.

It does not establish whether the underlying science is sound, whether a registered date can be met, why any date moved, or what any of it implies for a security. The expectation named here is a registered primary-completion expectation, which is not the date a result is published; the two differ systematically, by two to four months.
