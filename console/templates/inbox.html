Decision inbox

One item per decision, worst first, with every reason grouped under it. A decision carrying three reasons is one item with three rows beneath it, because three reasons to look at one belief is one piece of work, and splitting it into three rows turns a decision queue back into an alert feed.

Order is what needs a judgement today, then severity, then how many reasons the item carries. An item whose burn estimate is not usable is shown and never ranked: its evidence has to be repaired before it can be compared with the rest, so it carries no gap figure and no position worth reading.
